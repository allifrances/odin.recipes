Crock Pot Ribs

Description

The following recipe will teach you how to make a delicious plate of crock-pot-bbq ribs.

Follow along and enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Pork Ribs: You can use St. Louis style spare ribs OR baby back ribs (Either type works fine and tastes great!).
  • Barbeque Sauce: Use your favorite BBQ sauce for this recipe. Any kind works, store-bought or homemade.
  • Dry Rub: We use a mixture of paprika, brown sugar, red pepper flakes, and salt and pepper to make the dry rub seasoning for these ribs.
  • Cider Vinegar: This is optional, but I recommend adding it in if you're using a sweeter BBQ sauce. It adds a tanginess and just helps balance out the flavor.

Steps

  1. Prep ribs and set crock pot to LOW setting.
  2. Brush ribs with BBQ sauce and place them in the slow cooker.
  3. Pour sauce on top and add vinegar.
  4. Cover and cook 5-6 hours.
  5. Slice and serve with extra barbecue sauce, and enjoy!
